# Example: A Worked Week

A generic example for a family of **2 adults + 2 kids**, planning **5 cooking nights + 2
flex days**, cooking one batch for the whole table, high-protein lean-leaning, ~20–30 min
weeknights. Macros shown for one adult (the "primary eater") with a fat-first constraint.
This is illustrative — your `preferences.md` drives the real thing.

---

## Weekly Meal Plan — Week of Jul 20

### Mon — Turkey burgers
Griddled turkey patties on potato buns, avocado + tomato, hummus as the spread (lighter than
mayo). Raw snap peas and cucumbers on the side; kids get plain patties.
Table: cook all 8 patties (2 packs of 4).
Count math: 8 patties → table uses 5 (you 2, other adult 1, kids split 1) → 3 leftover.
You: 2 patties + 1 bun + tomato + hummus. ~690 cal / 58g P / 38g net C / 32g F — **fat's the
watch item**, so skip cheese and go thin on the spread; save avocado for Wed's tacos.
Leftovers: 3 patties → crumble into lunch bowls Thu–Sat.

### Tue — Pork chops + melted fennel & lemon
Sear chops, rest under foil, soften sliced fennel in the drippings with garlic + lemon.
Jasmine rice + roasted broccoli for the table.
Table: cook all 6 chops (2 packs of 3).
Count math: 6 chops → table uses ~4.5 → 1.5–2 leftover.
You: 1 chop (~6oz) + 1 cup rice + fennel. ~560 cal / 45g P / 46g net C / 18g F.
Leftovers: sliced cold pork → top-tier lunch-bowl protein Thu/Fri.

### Wed — Shrimp tacos
Mini tortillas, taco-seasoned shrimp, avocado, quick cucumber-tomato salsa with lime.
Table: ~2 lbs shrimp. Thaw shrimp in the fridge Tue morning.
You: 6oz shrimp + 4 mini tortillas + ½ avocado. ~540 cal / 42g P / 42g net C / 18g F.
Leftovers: extra shrimp → Thursday lunch bowl.

### Thu — Crispy gnocchi + charred squash + feta
Crisp gnocchi in a hot dry pan (no boiling), char zucchini + yellow squash alongside, toss
with lemon, herbs, feta over endive. Fry 1–2 eggs per adult on top for protein — kids love it.
You: 1 cup gnocchi + squash + 1oz feta + 2 eggs. ~570 cal / 24g P / 48g net C / 28g F —
**weak protein + heavy fat night**; front-load protein earlier and keep tonight's snack lean.
Leftovers: none — gnocchi doesn't hold, cook it all.

### Fri — Rotation bowl (leftover-driven)
Farro + leftover pork or turkey, snap peas, endive, greens, balsamic + a bold sauce.
You: ~1 cup grain + leftover protein + 2 cups veg. ~510 cal / 45g P / low fat.
Leftovers: clears the fridge before the weekend.

**Flex days (Sat, Sun):** remaining leftovers + one meal out.

---
**Leftover summary:** 3 turkey patties + ~2 pork chops = lunch proteins Thu–Sat; Fri dinner
finishes the rest. **Unused staples:** eggs, second bag of greens → weekend breakfasts.
